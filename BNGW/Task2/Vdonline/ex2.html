Scambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorire foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tbs butter
  • 2 Tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt buter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Heat butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot
  3. Pour in egg mixture. As eggs begin to set, gently pull the eggs across the pan with a spatula, forming large soft curds.
